SecurePass is an intelligent airport screening system that combines **biometric identity verification** and **AI-driven security interviews** to optimize passenger clearance and enhance threat detection.

---

## The Problem  
Airport security interviews today are **manual, slow, and difficult to scale**, especially with rising passenger traffic, and are prone to **bias and fatigue** from human officers.

---

## Our Solution  
SecurePass improves the workflow by:

- Verifying passengers using **biometrics** (face, fingerprint, iris)
- Conducting **automated AI security interviews**
- Detecting **non-literal responses** (jokes, metaphors, exaggerations flagged)
- Redirecting flagged cases for **secondary human screening**
- Reducing wait times and improving airport throughput
